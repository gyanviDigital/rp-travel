Chitwan one of the oldest National Park in Nepal where one can enjoy Jungle or Wild-Life.At Sauraha where most of the good lodge, hotels, and resorts located at the premises of National Park border rim. Chitwan is also a transit point for Pokhara, Lumbini and Ilam town, where famous Royal Bengal Tiger and one horned rhinoceros are spotted during Jungle tour with Tharu cultural program and birds watching the tour.
