    Day 01 - -Gorakhpur to Pokhara Via Lumbini 340 km 12-13 hrs


    As per your traveling choice, we will receive you at the Gorakhpur Bus Stand or Railway Station or Airport our representative will meet you welcome you and than transfer to Pokhara via Lumbini (Gautam Buddha birthplace) where your hotel is located. After hotel check in rest or you can explore the very famous Pokhara Lakeside.

    Day 02 - Day-Full Day Pokhara Sightseeing


    After Breakfast in hotel you will explore Pokhara.

  • VINDHYAWASHINI TEMPLE: This temple gives you the blissful feeling
  • SETI RIVER GORGE: WHITE RIVER.
  • BARAHI TEMPLE
  • GUPTESHWAR MAHADEV CAVE
  • DEVIS FALLS

    • After that back to hotel. After hotel check in rest or free activity. Over night stay at Pokhara.

    Day 03 - Transfer Pokhara to Jomsom


    Early Morning after breakfast we will move towards Jomsom enjoying the view of valley , snow covered mountain. After Hotel check in at Jomsom Rest. Overnight stay in hotel at Jomsom.

    Day 04 - Muktinath Darshan And Transfer to Pokhara


    Early morning Visit Muktinath Temple, Then come back to Hotel for breakfast, after breakfast in hotel drive towards Pokhara. After Hotel check in at Pokhara Rest. Overnight stay in hotel at Pokhara.

    Day 05 - Transfer to Kathmandu Via Manakamna


    After Breakfast in hotel move towards Kathmandu On the way you will visit Manakamna Devi temple via Cable Car Ride. After Cable car Ride continue journey towards Kathmandu. After Hotel check inn Rest. Overnight stay in hotel at Kathmandu. (Cable Car Ride charge will be extra).

    Day 06 - Full day Kathmandu Sightseeing


    After breakfast in hotel we will than explore the whole Kathmandu city.

  • Pashupatinath Temple: Kathmandu most visited place.
  • Boudhinath Stupa : It represent the Tibetan culture in Kathmandu.
  • Swayambhunath Stupa: One of the oldest Stupa in Nepal (13 century).
  • Kathmandu darbar square: One of the Darbar.
  • Patan darbar square: One of the three darbar.

    • After that back to hotel and after hotel check in rest or you can explore the Nepals famous Thamel market. Over night stay at Kathmandu.

    Day 07 - Kathmandu To Janakpur


    After Breakfast in hotel Drive towards Janakpur. After Reaching Janakpur You will visit Janki Temple. and Over night stay at Janakpur. This place is the perfect example of a blend of cultural aspect with the history of the country.

    Day 08 - Janakpur To Chitwan


    After breakfast in hotel we move towards Chitwan where your hotel is located. After hotel chek inn you can plan Jungle activity for next day. Overnight stay in hotel at Chitwan.

    Day 09 - Drop at Gorakhpur 245 km 6/-hrs


    After Breakfast in hotel you can enjoy Jungle Activity @direct payment basis.

  • Elephant Safari, Jeep Safari, Jungle Walk and Elephant Breeding center, Cannoing Ride.

    • on second half Transfer to Gorakhpur . After Reaching Gorakhpur You will visit Gorakhnaath Temple. Then you will be Drop at hotel or Railway Station/Airport.
