Pokhara Nepal Tour Package

Pokhara is a beautiful city located in the western part of Nepal. It is a popular tourist destination known for its stunning natural beauty, including panoramic views of the Himalayas, serene lakes, and lush green hills. Here's some information about a Pokhara tour in Nepal. Pokhara is a place of natural beauty and adventure, providing a refreshing experience for travelers. Whether you're seeking relaxation, trekking, or cultural exploration, Pokhara has something to offer.

Location of Pokhara City in Nepal:

Pokhara is situated about 200 kilometers west of Kathmandu, the capital city of Nepal. It is the gateway to the Annapurna mountain range and a starting point for many popular trekking routes, including the famous Annapurna Circuit and Annapurna Base Camp Trek.

Annapurna Mountain Range

Lakes in Pokhara:

Pokhara is known as the "City of Lakes" due to its numerous lakes. The most famous one is Phewa Lake, a picturesque freshwater lake where you can enjoy boating and witness the reflection of the Annapurna and Dhaulagiri mountain ranges on calm days. You can also visit other lakes like Begnas Lake and Rupa Lake, which offer a tranquil environment.

Adventurous activities in Pokhara:

Pokhara is a hub for adventure enthusiasts. You can go paragliding and experience the thrill of flying over the city with the stunning Himalayan backdrop. Additionally activities like zip-lining, bungee jumping, and ultralight flights are available for adrenaline junkies.

Trekking and hiking in Pokhara:

Pokhara serves as a base for various trekking routes in the Annapurna region. You can embark on treks of different durations and difficulty levels, ranging from short hikes to multi-day expeditions. Some popular treks include the Annapurna Base Camp Trek, Poon Hill Trek, and Mardi Himal Trek.

Cultural and historical sites in Pokhara:

Pokhara also offers cultural and historical attractions. You can visit the International Mountain Museum to learn about the mountains, their climbers and the local culture. Additionally Bindhyabasini Temple, Devi's Fall, and the Old Bazaar are worth exploring.

Food and shopping in Pokhara:

Pokhara has a wide range of restaurants and cafes that offer both local and international cuisine. You can taste traditional Nepali dishes like dal bhat (rice and lentil soup), momo (dumplings), and Newari cuisine. The Lakeside area is a popular shopping destination offering a variety of handicrafts, textiles, and souvenirs.
