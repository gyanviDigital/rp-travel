Muktinath Tour Package

Muktinath is a sacred pilgrimage site located in the Mustang district of Nepal. It holds religious significance for both Hindus and Buddhists and is considered one of the 108 Divya Desams (holy abodes of Lord Vishnu) in Hinduism. The temple at Muktinath is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and is believed to grant liberation (moksha or muktinath) from the cycle of birth and death.
The Muktinath tour is a popular pilgrimage journey undertaken by devotees, especially from Nepal and India. It typically involves traveling to the Muktinath Temple and other nearby religious sites. The journey to Muktinath usually starts from the town of Jomsom, which is accessible by a short flight from Pokhara.

Shree Muktinath Temple Tour

From Jomsom, visitors hire a local jeep to reach Muktinath. The trekking route passes through picturesque landscapes, including the Kali Gandaki Gorge, the world's deepest gorge, and offers breathtaking views of the Himalayas. The tour offers stunning views of the Himalayas, including the Annapurna and Dhaulagiri mountain ranges. Along the way, travelers can also explore the unique culture and traditions of the people living in the Mustang region.

Best Attraction to Muktinath Temple :

Muktinath Temple:

The main attraction of the tour, this ancient temple is situated at an altitude of 3,710 meters and is revered by both Hindus and Buddhists.

Jomsom:

It is the district headquarters of Mustang and serves as the gateway to Muktinath. The town is famous for its apple orchards and is a popular stop for trekkers.

Kagbeni:

An ancient village situated on the banks of the Kali Gandaki River, known for its unique architecture and cultural heritage.

Marpha:

Known for its apple brandy and beautiful white-washed houses, Marpha is a picturesque village en route to Muktinath.

Jharkot and Ranipauwa:

These are two villages near Muktinath, where visitors can experience local culture and traditions.

Muktinath Trek:

Trekkers have the option to undertake a short trek from Jomsom to Muktinath, which provides an opportunity to enjoy the breathtaking landscapes of the Mustang region.

Best Time to Travel Muktinath

The best time to embark on the Muktinath tour is during the spring (February to May) and autumn (September to November) seasons when the weather is pleasant and the skies are clear. During the winter months, the region experiences cold temperatures and heavy snowfall, making the journey difficult.
